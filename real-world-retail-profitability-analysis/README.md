# Retail Revenue & Profitability Analysis

# Context
I have been operating retail store operations for 2+years, below is an analysis from the store’s sales data to understand seasonal trend to improve profitability & revenue.

# Objective
The aim is to observe category performance, seasonal trends & profit variance to increase the revenue.

# Business Question
1. Which months makes the most money?
2. Which categories makes the most money?
3. Which categories shows high sales volume but low margins?
4. How stable is the business across observed period?

# Observations
- High revenue were recorded in August & October, indicating a festival rich demand. However, the revenue & profit’s in other months varied by strong numbers, indicating a low customer footfall resulting in lower revenue collection.
- Categories across months performed relative similar, suggesting a stable consumer demand with periodic volatility.
- Maintenance Service has high volume but comparatively lower margins, a missed opportunity which if worked on can give meaningful improvement in profitability.
- Protective accessories & Charging accessories have performed relatively better in margin collection, they are highly stable across months with constant demand.

# Decisions
- Focus on brand awareness & local influencer marketing to improve consumer awareness. This will stabilize the sales across volatile months resulting in better revenue collection over the time.
- Festival months attract high visitors resulting in increased sales, it is an opportunity to renegotiate pricing with suppliers to get better deals and increase margins.
- Restructure the pricing of maintenance services, this will benefit the business with meaningful improvement in profits.

# Outcome
Overall profitability and revenue of the firm increased by 19% over a period of 6months.

# Data Used
- transactions.csv: Transaction-level sales data
- product_master.csv: Standardized cost reference by category

# Tools Used
- Excel for digitizing data and aggregations
- SQL fro motnhly trend and profitability analysis
- Power BI for executive level dashboard
  
# Data Information
- This sales data was digitized from a handwritten, raw sales accounting notebook.
- For reliability purpose the sales data was limited to six months and has been standardized into categories.

# Recomendations
- Prioritize inventory, shelf space and promotions for consistently high margin categories
- Review pricing and sourcing strategies for high volume but lower margin categories to improve profitability
- Monitor trends alongside revenue to avoid revenue led but profit negative decisions
